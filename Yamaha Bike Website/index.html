R15 v3 Design

Two wheels, one engine, no limit

Aerodynamic LED Tail and Headlight
Aerodynamic LED Tail and Headlight

While the sharp design assists in overall airflow, the slim twin-eye LED headlights ensure a more offers efficient and superior lighting even for night rides.

140mm Super Wide Radial Rear Tyre
140mm Super Wide Radial Rear Tyre

66H radial tyre has been adopted at the rear with flexible side for better grip while cornering and superior shock absorbing ensure a more comfortable ride.

In-Built Side Stand Engine cut-off Switch
In-Built Side Stand Engine cut-off Switch

The days that you ride off without your side stand retracted are behind you. With the In-built side stand engine cut-off switch,remove side stand during those immediate rides.

Multi-function LCD Instrument Cluster with Gear Shift Indicator
Multi-function LCD Instrument Cluster with Gear Shift Indicator

Personalized string of animated text. Keeps you informed of all the essentials: gear shift and gear position indicator, VVA indicator, in addition to speed, tachometer and trip meter.

Aluminium Swingarm
Aluminium Swingarm

R15 gets a sporty, stable handling a machine emphasizing riding feel. A linked-type Monocross rear suspension is adopted to accommodate the powerful engine character and a wide rear tyre are adopted for optimum balance.

Deltabox Frame
Deltabox Frame

The new R15 adopts a new Deltabox frame with superb rigidity balance. With a Deltabox frame, the top and bottom of the head pipe and the pivot points form a triangle, hence the “Deltabox” gives it excellent rigidity balance.
